# Privacy Policy for Zenith Pro

**Last Updated:** April 29, 2026

Zenith Pro ("we," "our," or "the App") is a premium, offline-first productivity application. Your privacy is our core product. This policy outlines how we handle (or rather, do not handle) your data.

## 1. 100% Offline Nature
Zenith Pro is designed to be completely offline. 
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your data never leaves your device. We do not maintain any servers to store your notes, tasks, or PINs.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use any analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics) to track your usage.

## 2. Data Encryption
To ensure that even if someone gains physical access to your device, they cannot read your data, Zenith Pro employs **AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard)**.
- **Local Encryption:** All Notes and Tasks are encrypted before being written to the device's storage.
- **Master PIN:** We use **PBKDF2** (Password-Based Key Derivation Function 2) with a unique local salt to derive an encryption key from your 4-digit PIN. 
- **Zero Knowledge:** We have "Zero Knowledge" of your PIN. If you lose your PIN, the data cannot be recovered.

## 3. Permissions
Zenith Pro requests minimal permissions:
- **Storage:** To save and load your encrypted vault locally.
- **Audio:** To generate synthetic brown noise (processed locally on-device).

## 4. Third-Party Data Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not—and cannot—share, sell, or trade your personal information with any third parties.
